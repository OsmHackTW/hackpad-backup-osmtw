Safe Food Taiwan

Aims

  • Mapping food supplies/factories on the Map for encouraging more citizens to report illegal food supplies/factories
  • To provide citizen a transparent reporting mechanism which government side has to immediately respond to the requests from citizen side
  • To push food supplies/factories to open ingredients which can be used to trace unsafe/poisonous materials
