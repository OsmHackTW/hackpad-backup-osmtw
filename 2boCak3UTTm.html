Tagging schema

  • 建築物
    • 確定沒有毀損 building = yes, explode:reviewed=yes
    • 部分毀損 building = damaged, explode:damage=yes
    • 完全毀損 building = collapsed, explode:damage=yes
  • 道路
    • 障礙 barrier=debris, explode:damage=yes (一個點)
    • 某一段不能通行 status=impassable, explode:damage=yes
  • 一大片毀損 landuse=brownfield, explode:damage=yes
