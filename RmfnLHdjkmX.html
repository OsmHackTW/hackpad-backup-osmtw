Usage of OSM data in disaster relief field works

This hackpad is to collect usage cases of relief teams in disaster field works.  

  • Use case in Haiti earthquake http://wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/Talk:WikiProject_Haiti#Fairfax_County_Urban_Search_.26_Rescue_Team_Using_Garmin_downloads

Relief Team

Tzu Chi

  • Use OSM to indicate the current place of TzuChi relief team for people on the web.

Red Cross

  • Red Cross use it for "literally the full gamut of what you can do with [geographic information systems] right now" http://www.theatlantic.com/technology/archive/2015/05/the-mapmakers-helping-nepal/392228/

KLL (Kathmandu Living Labs)

  • KLL received printed map using OSM as primary layer from the Department of National Defence, Canada. http://kathmandulivinglabs.org/blog/nepal-earthquake-report-from-kll-situation-room-day-10-may-5/
  • http://kathmandulivinglabs.org/blog/nepal-earthquake-report-from-kll-situation-room-day-11-may-6/
